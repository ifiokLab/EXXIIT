EXXiiT is a platform to help you create lasting memories in honor of your loved ones. Our website or app helps you plan, organize, and execute your loved ones final rites. The legacy feature helps you create online memorials to virtually participate, attend, or contribute to a loved one's memorial.

Anyone can donate money in lieu of lighting a candle, giving a flower, watering a plant, sending a sympathy card, or donating to a partner charity to demonstrate support so that their legacy may serve as a source of light and inspiration for those who knew them in life and those who never had the chance. As a token of our gratitude, we will have a physical tree planted in their honor. Your contribution will ensure that our app and/or website remain free for other bereaved families.

Your participation is essential. By liking, commenting, writing a short tribute, or simply sharing the post with other loved ones, you are ensuring the soul of your loved one's life keeps on beaming and inspiring others who may come across it. Their life and story may inspire others. You never know whose life you may inspire or change with simple words from your heart.

Anyone can create an account in honor of their loved ones, a celebrity, or simply search for an already created memorial page and participate by writing a tribute, commenting, or contributing using our memorial gift features to honor a loved one. Any memorial page that has not received any public interactions other than your account will be purged after 3 to 7 days of creation. To avoid this, be sure to share any memorial pages you create in your social media groups to garner support and interactions to validate your page.

You may earn a verified badge based on the number of memorial pages you have created in the lifespan of your account and how many others have contributed to those pages you have created, either by commenting, adding tributes, or through memorial gifts. Our system's algorithm determines and identifies your popularity and the honors you bestow on others. Lastly, the system also verifies the active followers and the interactions and reactions you have received on your personal page connected to any memorial pages created.
